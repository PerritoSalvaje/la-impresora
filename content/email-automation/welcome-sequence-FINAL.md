## SETUP EN BEEHIIV — PASO A PASO

1. Beehiiv → Automations → "Welcome new subscribers"
2. Trigger: "Subscriber is confirmed"
3. Filter: "First time subscribed" (evita spam a re-suscriptores)
4. Add Email Action × 7 con estos delays:
   - Email 1: Send immediately
   - Email 2: Wait 2 days → Send
   - Email 3: Wait 2 days → Send
   - Email 4: Wait 2 days → Send
   - Email 5: Wait 3 days → Send
   - Email 6: Wait 3 days → Send
   - Email 7: Wait 2 days → Send

5. Configuración global:
   - Sender name: "La Impresora"
   - Sender email: hola@laimpresora.io (configurar en Beehiiv → Settings → Sending Email)
   - Track opens: ON
   - Track clicks: ON
   - Reply-to: hola@laimpresora.io

6. Después de 7 emails → automation termina. La rama "engaged ≥3 opens" la handlea Academy automation (otra automation).
